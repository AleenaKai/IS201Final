About Me

My name is Aleena Kai and I am a food enthusiast who loves to cook and spend time with family. Here, I have compiled some of my 'ohanas favorite family recipes that have been passed down from generation to generation. I believe that food is not just about nourishing our bodies, but also about creating memories and building relationships. I hope that these recipes will inspire you to get into the kitchen and start cooking, and that they will become a part of your own family traditions. So come on in, take a look around, and find something delicious to share with your loved ones!

The recipes documented on this website come from my great-grandmother May Au, my tutu Valerie Thiim, my papa Umi Kai, and my mom Kapua Kai.
