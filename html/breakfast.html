Breakfast

German Pancakes

    • 6 eggs
    • 1 cup flour
    • 1 cup milk
    • 4 Tbsp butter
    • 1 tsp vanilla
    • 1 cup fresh or frozen blueberries

Place butter in 13 by 9 inch pan, put in oven and heat to 425 degrees farenheit. Place all other ingredients in a blender (except blueberries) and blend until smooth, scraping the sides if necessary. When butter is melted remove and carefully pour batter evenly in pan. Sprinkle with blueberries, return to oven and bake for 22-30 minutes.


Egg Casserole

    • 2 Tbsp oil
    • 9 eggs
    • 1 small onion, chopped
    • 1⁄2 pkg of frozen hash browns
    • 2 cup baby spinach
    • 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
    • 1⁄2 cup milk

Preheat oven to 350 degrees farenheit, and grease a 13 by 9 inch pan. Heat pan on medium heat. Add oil and heat through. Add onion, red pepper, and saut√® for a few minutes until softened. Add baby spinach, season with salt and pepper and mix until wilted. Remove from heat and set aside. In large bowl add eggs, milk, and mix until fluffy. Add hashbrowns and season with salt and pepper. Add vegetable mixture, cheese, and mix well. Pour into dish and bake for 25-35 minutes until eggs are set.
